# Privacy Policy for Y2Notes

**Last Updated:** April 11, 2026

## Overview

Y2Notes is a professional note-taking application designed for iPad that prioritizes your privacy. This privacy policy explains how Y2Notes handles your information.

## Our Commitment

**We believe your notes are private and belong to you.** Y2Notes is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- ✅ **All your data stays on your device** by default
- ✅ **No analytics or tracking** - we don't collect usage data
- ✅ **No accounts required** - use the app without signing up
- ✅ **Optional cloud sync** - you control if and where data is backed up

## Data Collection

### Information We DO NOT Collect

Y2Notes does **not** collect:
- Personal identifying information
- Usage statistics or analytics
- Device information or diagnostics
- Crash reports (unless you explicitly share them)
- Advertising identifiers
- Location data

### Information Stored Locally

Y2Notes stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

1. **Your Notes and Drawings**
   - Handwritten notes created with Apple Pencil
   - Text content and formatting
   - Attached images and PDF annotations
   - Study flashcards and progress

2. **App Preferences**
   - Theme selection
   - Tool configurations
   - App settings

3. **Optional Audio Recordings**
   - If you use the audio recording feature, recordings are stored locally on your device
   - Microphone access requires your explicit permission

## Optional Cloud Sync (Google Drive)

If you choose to enable Google Drive synchronization:

### What We Access
- **Read/Write Access**: Y2Notes needs permission to read and write files in your Google Drive to sync your notes
- **Scope**: Access is limited to files created by Y2Notes only
- **Authentication**: Uses Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication (we never see your Google password)

### What We Do NOT Do
- We do **not** upload your data to our servers
- We do **not** access other files in your Google Drive
- We do **not** share your data with third parties
- We do **not** analyze or process your synced data

### Your Control
- Sync is entirely optional - the app works fully without it
- You can disconnect Google Drive at any time from Settings
- Disconnecting stops all sync but keeps local data intact
- You can delete synced files from your Google Drive at any time

## Third-Party Services

### Google Drive (Optional)
- If you enable sync, your data is transmitted to Google Drive
- Google's privacy policy applies to data stored in your Google Drive: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- The connection is encrypted using industry-standard TLS

### Apple Services
- Y2Notes uses Apple's PencilKit framework for drawing
- Drawing data is processed entirely on-device
- No data is sent to Apple servers by Y2Notes

## Children's Privacy

Y2Notes does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not have features specifically targeting children under 13.

## Data Security

- All local data is protected by iOS encryption
- Your device passcode protects access to the app
- Optional cloud sync uses encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS)
- We recommend enabling device encryption and using a strong passcode

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All your data is accessible through the app interface
- **Export**: You can export notes as PDF at any time
- **Delete**: Deleting notes removes them permanently from your device
- **Portability**: Export your data to standard formats (PDF, images)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect app improvements. We will notify users of significant changes through:
- App update notes
- In-app notification
- Updated policy date at the top of this document

## Data Retention

- Local data remains on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- Cloud synced data remains in your Google Drive until you delete it
