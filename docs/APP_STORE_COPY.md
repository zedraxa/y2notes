## Description

**Professional note-taking with PDF annotation and spaced-repetition study**

Y2Notes is a focused, professional iPad app designed for students and professionals who take their notes seriously. Built specifically for Apple Pencil, Y2Notes combines three essential features into one powerful tool:

### ✍️ Natural Handwriting
- Professional-grade drawing engine powered by Apple's PencilKit
- Pressure and tilt-sensitive ink for natural writing
- Multiple pen types and customizable colors
- Smooth, responsive performance on Apple Pencil

### 📄 PDF Annotation
- Import and annotate PDFs directly
- Mark up textbooks, papers, and documents
- Keep your notes and documents together
- Export annotated PDFs to share

### 🎓 Smart Study System
- Built-in flashcard creation from your notes
- Spaced-repetition algorithm for efficient learning
- Track your study progress over time
- Perfect for exam preparation
